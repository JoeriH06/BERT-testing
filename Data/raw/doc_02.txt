Circular Tourism Lab

Project ID: CTL-2025-04
Start date: 2025-09-01

The Circular Tourism Lab explores how circular entrepreneurship can be applied in tourism areas around the North Sea. The project examines waste reduction, sustainable energy use and water reuse in tourism businesses.

This project is linked to HZ Kenniscentrum Kusttoerisme. Researchers involved include Sophie Martens and Ruben Dekker. The project works together with tourism companies and municipalities in Zeeland.
